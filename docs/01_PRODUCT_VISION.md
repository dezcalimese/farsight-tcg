# Farsight

### Product Vision — Pokémon TCG Intelligence Feed

## What this is

A constant, ambient feed of Pokémon TCG market intelligence — trending cards, sealed product movement, restocks, and news — distilled into short summaries delivered where people already are (text, email, Discord). Personal portfolio tracking sits on top as a layer for people who want their own holdings folded into the feed, but the feed itself is the product. You don't have to hold a single card to get value from it.

The wedge: nobody has to actively check five sites, three Discords, and a Twitter list to know what's happening in this market. This tells them.

## Who it's for

Me, first. Then anyone getting into Pokémon card investing who wants signal without the daily grind of monitoring it themselves — the same person who'd want a "market open" email for stocks but for TCG.

## The one metric

**Weekly Active Recipients** — number of people who open/read a digest (email, text, or Discord) in a given week.

Not portfolio value tracked, not alerts fired, not cards in the DB. If the digest isn't something people actually read, nothing else in this product matters. Everything else is in service of this number going up and staying up.

## What "done" looks like for v1

A person can sign up, pick a channel (email/text/Discord), and start receiving a genuinely useful periodic summary — trending cards this week, notable restocks, price movers, one or two news items — without configuring anything. Portfolio tracking and custom alerts are there for people who want to go deeper, but the zero-config digest is what proves the product works.

## Non-goals (for now)

- This is not a trading platform — no buying, selling, or payment processing
- This is not a grading service or authentication tool
- This is not trying to compete with TCGPlayer/PriceCharting as a pricing database — it curates and summarizes, it doesn't replace them
